## Overall Evaluation

Here you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program, helping the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following categories:

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment at AWS Vietnam is very friendly and supportive. The team members are always ready to help when I encounter difficulties. The workspace is comfortable. I think if there were more team-building activities, it would help interns connect and integrate faster.

### 2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin

The mentor provides very dedicated guidance, explains clearly, and encourages me to find ways to solve problems before asking for help. I highly appreciate how the mentor is always patient in answering my basic questions.

### 3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major

The assigned work is quite suitable with the knowledge I learned at school, while also helping me approach many real AWS services that I previously only knew through theory.

### 4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities

I learned a lot of new things: how to use AWS services, infrastructure design thinking, teamwork skills, and how to write worklogs.

### 5. Company Culture & Team Spirit

Everyone in the team works seriously but is very fun and supportive of each other. I can feel the good team spirit.

## Some Additional Questions

### Most Satisfying Experience

Being guided by the dedicated mentor and having the opportunity to practice directly on AWS Free Tier.

### Things You Think the Company Should Improve

It can be improved by organizing more detailed onboarding sessions from the beginning, helping interns understand the processes, tools, and team expectations. Additionally, the company can create more opportunities for interns to attend review, demo, or internal knowledge sharing sessions, thereby better understanding actual work processes.

### Recommend to Friends

Yes. I would recommend friends to join because of the good environment, learning opportunities, and enthusiastic mentor support.

## Suggestions & Expectations

### Increase Practical Workshop Sessions

I suggest the company organize practical workshop sessions focusing on real topics such as: AWS infrastructure deployment, cost optimization, and troubleshooting. This will help interns approach knowledge systematically and have direct guidance.

### Post-Internship Support

I hope to maintain contact with the mentor and team after the internship to continue learning, sharing experiences, and updating future cooperation opportunities.

### Expand the Internship Program

If possible, I hope this internship program will be expanded so that more students have the opportunity to experience a professional working environment like at AWS Vietnam.

### Acknowledgments

I would like to sincerely thank the entire FCJ team and AWS Vietnam Company for creating conditions, supporting, and accompanying me throughout the internship. This is truly a meaningful and valuable experience.
