## Introduction

On 23/05/2026, the **FCAJ Community Day** event was successfully held with the theme "Connect, Share & Real Tech Practice". This was a meetup organized by the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) community, bringing together students and technology enthusiasts who are on the journey to master Cloud, AI and DevOps.

The event focused on connecting like-minded people, sharing practical knowledge and inspiring young people who are starting their journey in the technology industry.

## Event Objectives

The main objectives of the event include:

- **Connect FCAJ community** and technology-enthusiastic students
- **Share knowledge** about Cloud, AI and DevOps from practical perspectives
- **Inspire** and guide career orientation for students
- **Build networking** in the technology industry

## Main Activities

### 1. Networking & Connection

- Meet and exchange with FCAJ community members
- Share learning and working experiences in the Cloud industry
- Build relationships with like-minded people

### 2. Knowledge Sharing

- Topics about AWS services and best practices
- Knowledge sharing sessions from experienced speakers
- Hands-on demonstrations with real technology
