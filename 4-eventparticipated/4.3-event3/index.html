Event 3

FCAJ Meetup 2026 – Journey from Student to Cloud, DevOps & Data Professional

Event Information

Event NameFCAJ Meetup 2026 – Journey from Student to Cloud, DevOps & Data Professional
Time13/06/2026
Location26th Floor, Bitexco Building, No. 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City
RoleAttendee

Introduction

On 13/06/2026, the FCAJ Meetup 2026 event was successfully held with the participation of many students and young people passionate about technology. This was an extremely useful sharing session, providing practical and relatable perspectives from speakers who are currently working in Cloud, AI, DevOps, and Data fields.

The meetup focused on career development journey from the perspective of those who came before, helping students gain a clearer view of the path ahead.

Event Objectives

The main objectives of the event include:

  • Sharing real career journeys in the Technology industry from the perspective of people working at large companies
  • Guiding skill development roadmap in Cloud, DevOps, and Data Analytics fields
  • Introducing working culture at multinational corporations (MNC)
  • Inspiring and guiding career orientation for students who are still studying

Speakers

The event was honored to welcome experienced industry professionals as speakers:

  • Kien & Tho – FCAJ Representatives (First Cloud AI Journey)
  • Danh Hoang Hieu Nghi – AI Engineer, AWS Community Builder, AWS Student Builder Group Leader
  • Trong H. Truong – DevOps Engineer at Endava Vietnam
  • Dat Pham – Data Analytics Engineer
  • Cuong Nguyen – Process Engineer

Detailed Content

1. Journey from First Cloud AI Journey to AWS Partner

Speaker Danh Hoang Hieu Nghi shared his inspiring personal journey – from a student starting to participate in the First Cloud AI Journey program, to becoming an AWS Community Builder and AWS Partner.

He emphasized a very meaningful quote:

“Getting the job is just a beginning”

From his experience, he encouraged young people:

  • Build personal brand through LinkedIn
  • Actively participate in tech communities
  • Do many real projects to gain experience

2. What Does a Real DevOps Engineer Do?

Speaker Trong H. Truong from Endava Vietnam brought a practical perspective on the DevOps Engineer role.

He analyzed clearly the differences between theory and reality in DevOps work, while sharing hard-learned lessons through “Things I learned the hard way”.

Especially, he provided a learning roadmap distilled from real experience:

PhaseContent
FoundationLinux, Networking, Git
ToolsCI/CD, Containers
CloudAWS, Azure, GCP

3. Data Analytics Engineer at Multinational Corporation

Speakers Dat Pham and Cuong Nguyen shared valuable experiences working at Kamereo and Colgate-Palmolive.

Key points emphasized:

  • Role of data analysis in supporting business decision-making
  • “Storytelling with Data” skills – how to tell stories through data
  • Career development model:
Follower → Learner → Problem Solver → System Thinker → Super Star

4. Working Culture at Multinational Corporation

Speakers shared about working culture at MNC:

  • Professional recruitment process at multinational companies
  • No-Blame Post-Mortem culture – focus on lessons learned instead of blaming
  • Caring & Inclusive culture – caring and inclusive work environment
  • Importance of critical thinking, communication, and lifelong learning

Lessons Learned

Through the meetup, I gained much useful knowledge:

  • Foundation is the key: Need to build a solid foundation (Linux, Networking, Git) before learning advanced technologies
  • DevOps is a mindset: DevOps is not just tools but also a way of thinking about systems
  • Combine technical and business skills: Data Analytics needs to combine technical skills with business thinking to create value
  • Culture matters: Corporate culture is equally important as professional skills
  • Participate in communities: AWS Student Builder Group, Community Builder are communities worth joining

Personal Action Plan

From what I learned, I have planned for myself:

  1. Build a learning roadmap for AWS and DevOps suitable for myself
  2. Complete LinkedIn and GitHub profile to build personal brand
  3. Practice deploying projects on AWS to accumulate real experience
  4. Apply AI to support development like Amazon Q, Claude and other tools
  5. Prepare soft skills and necessary mindset to be ready to apply to MNC

Personal Impressions

Participating in FCAJ Meetup 2026 was an extremely valuable experience for me. The speakers not only shared professional knowledge but also told real stories, including difficulties and lessons from failures.

That helped me gain a clearer view of the career path in Cloud, DevOps, and Data. I was particularly impressed by the sincerity and enthusiasm of the speakers. The event not only provided professional knowledge but also strongly inspired the spirit of continuous learning and the importance of building personal brand.

Conclusion

FCAJ Meetup 2026 was truly a meaningful meetup, helping the younger generation have clearer direction on the technology career path. I would like to send my sincere thanks to the Organizing Committee and all speakers for taking the time to share valuable knowledge and experience.

The meetup has ended but the lessons and inspiration will accompany me on the road ahead.
